''MARHAM'' is an NGO which  comes into effect to make a change in the life of voiceless stray animals , give utmost care to their needs , safeguard them , provide shelter, and give them their most awaited love and care.....

About Us

Our Mission

The mission of  MARHAM is to sow the seeds of empathy for voiceless, compassion for animals and care for the environment in the hearts of children, so as to prevent cruelty to all living beings. We accomplish our mission by pursuing a three-pronged approach:

  1. Animal Helpline and website : By running a very active animal helpline  and website that organizes rescue of animals in distress; counsels pet owners on a variety of pets and stray animal issues; helps in adoption of abandoned pets and other animal welfare activities.
  2. Workshops & Community Outreach Programs: By conducting humane education workshops, nukkad natak , documentaries, nature walks and  camps in schools; organizing awareness talks and presentations in colleges along with other community outreach programs at work places.
  3. Social media campaign: Through rigorous and active participation on social media which will mainly lay emphasis on the suffering and pain and the visuals through our community outreach.

Our Vision

  1. According to estimates approx.35 million stray dogs live in India, even though it's illegal to kill them but a fine of Rs 50 is what the punishment in the Prevention of cruelty to animals act 1960 and after it the culprit is free from the heinous crime......
  2. The Indian Constitution in its Article 51A establishes that it is the duty of every citizen of India "To protect and improve the natural environment including forests, lakes, rivers, and wildlife, and to have compassion for living creatures"
  3. MARHAM visions to lay a strong emphasis on the strengthening of animal protections act and make it highly punishable. We strongly believe reforms in laws is a must to ensure safety to stray  animals .
  4. MARHAM envisions an India where where people treat each other with empathy, animals with compassion and care for the environment.
  5.  We at MARHAM  strive to achieve this through the medium of humane education.
